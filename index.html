About Kumasi High School

Established in 1964, Kumasi High School (KUHIS) is one of Ghana's leading Senior High Schools, located in the heart of the Ashanti Region.

Kumasi High School commonly known as KUHIS, is a Category A public all-boys senior high school located in Kumasi, in the Ashanti Region of Ghana. Established in 1962 by S. K. Amoah, the school has developed a strong reputation for academic excellence, particularly in business education.

KUHIS is noted for its performance in both academics and extracurricular activities. The school has a history of success in the Ashanti Region inter-school competitions and Ashanti Region super zonals.

Kumasi High School is tied for the most wins and current runner-up in the Luv FM High School Debate. Kumasi High School also won the inaugural USA Ambassador Business Quiz and is a one-time winner of the Ashanti Regional Science and Maths Quiz, where KUHIS triumphed over city rivals Opoku Ware School and Prempeh College in 2024.

Our Vision is to be seen as a model learning institution recognized globally for providing academic and moral excellence.

Our Mission is to equip our students with excellent academic and moral training to help prepare them fit into the world of work.

Our Academic Programs

Kumasi High School offers five main programs of study as approved by the Ghana Education Service

General Science

Comprehensive science program preparing students for careers in medicine, engineering, and technology.

  • Physics
  • Chemistry
  • Biology
  • Elective Mathematics

Agricultural Science

Hands-on agricultural training combined with scientific principles for modern farming and agribusiness.

  • Animal Husbandry
  • Agriculture

Business

Comprehensive business education preparing students for careers in accounting, management, and entrepreneurship.

  • Accounting
  • Business Management
  • Economics
  • Costing

General Arts

Diverse arts program covering humanities, social sciences, and languages for well-rounded education.

  • Literature in English
  • History
  • Government
  • Christian Religious Studies

Visual Arts

Creative program developing artistic skills and appreciation for careers in fine arts and design.

  • General Knowledge in Art
  • Graphic Design
  • Picture Making
  • Sculpture

Admission Requirements & Process

Admission to Kumasi High School is through the Computerized School Selection and Placement System (CSSPS) managed by the Ghana Education Service.

Requirements:

  • Successful completion of Basic Education (B.E.C.E)
  • Aggregate score meeting the cut-off point for selected program
  • Good conduct report from previous school

Program-Specific Requirements:

Each program has specific subject requirements. Please contact the admissions office for detailed information.
