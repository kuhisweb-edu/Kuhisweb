Prefects Council

Student leaders dedicated to service, discipline, and excellence

Leading by Example

The Prefects Council of Kumasi High School comprises dedicated student leaders who serve as a bridge between the student body and school administration. Elected through a rigorous process, these young leaders embody the school's core values of knowledge, discipline, and excellence.

Our prefects are responsible for maintaining discipline, organizing student activities, and representing student interests in school governance. They undergo leadership training and serve as role models for their peers.
